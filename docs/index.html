How we work

A structured engagement, every time

Every engagement follows our tested methodology — no shortcuts, no surprises. Four quality gates ensure nothing falls through the cracks.

Scoping & Authorization

We define the target environment, rules of engagement, and success criteria before a single packet is sent.

Reconnaissance

Passive and active enumeration to map your attack surface — assets, services, technologies, and exposure.

Testing

Manual exploitation following OWASP, PTES, and MITRE ATT&CK frameworks. Every finding is confirmed — no automated-scanner noise.

Reporting

Technical report plus executive summary. Each finding includes reproduction steps, business impact, and a prioritized remediation recommendation.

Retest

After you remediate, we retest Critical and High findings at no additional charge within 60 days and issue a delta report confirming resolution.

Business-context findings

Every finding is framed in terms of actual business impact — not just a CVSS score. Your leadership can understand the risk without reading the technical details.

Evidence you can trust

SHA-256 verified evidence with full chain of custody. Every claim in our reports is backed by documented, reproducible proof.

Remediation partnership

We don't disappear after delivery. We answer questions during your remediation window and retest critical findings at no added cost.
