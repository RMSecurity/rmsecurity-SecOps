## Stage 1 — Report Delivery Confirmed

- [ ] Technical report delivered (PDF, password-protected)
- [ ] Executive summary delivered (PDF, password-protected)
- [ ] Remediation tracker delivered (XLSX, pre-populated)
- [ ] Report password sent via separate channel (not email with the report)
- [ ] Client confirmed receipt of all deliverables
- [ ] **Gate 4 checklist completed and passed**

## Stage 2 — Debrief

- [ ] Debrief call completed
- [ ] All client questions answered or logged for follow-up
- [ ] Remediation guidance provided for all critical and high findings
- [ ] Retest window and process explained to client
- [ ] Retest window end date communicated: _______________

## Stage 3 — Financial Close

- [ ] Final invoice sent
- [ ] Final invoice paid / payment plan documented
- [ ] Engagement revenue recorded in financial tracker

## Stage 4 — Internal Closure

- [ ] `engagement.json` updated with closure date and final status
- [ ] Finding count and severity breakdown recorded in internal metrics
- [ ] Any new techniques, tools, or findings of interest logged in `32-KNOWLEDGE-BASE`
- [ ] Lessons learned documented (what went well / what to improve)
- [ ] Assessment VM wiped and rebuilt if used for this engagement
- [ ] Evidence on assessment machine confirmed wiped

## Stage 5 — Evidence Archive

- [ ] All evidence in encrypted store (`04-evidence/`)
- [ ] Chain of custody manifest finalized and SHA-256 verified
- [ ] Retention period end date calculated and noted: _______________
  *(1 year for evidence, 3 years for reports and manifests, 7 years for contracts)*
- [ ] Engagement folder access confirmed restricted

## Stage 6 — Relationship

- [ ] Client satisfaction check-in sent (email or call)
- [ ] Client asked about upcoming security needs
- [ ] Client added to newsletter / update list (if consented)
- [ ] Referral opportunity assessed
