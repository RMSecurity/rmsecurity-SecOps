## 1. Introductions (5 min)

- rmsecurity team introduction and roles
- Client team introduction — confirm decision-makers present
- Confirm the correct people are on the call (technical + management contacts)

## 2. Engagement Overview (10 min)

- Confirm scope: review in-scope assets from the ROE
- Confirm out-of-scope items
- Confirm engagement type and objectives
- Clarify any scope questions before testing begins

## 3. Testing Window Confirmation (5 min)

- Confirm start date and end date
- Confirm permitted testing hours and time zone
- Confirm weekend testing authorization (if applicable)
- Confirm any blackout periods (planned maintenance, critical business periods)

## 4. Technical Logistics (10 min)

- rmsecurity egress IP address(es) — client may need to whitelist
- VPN or jump host access (if required) — confirm credentials work
- Contact method during testing (email / Slack / Teams / phone)
- What to do if an alert fires during testing (expected behavior vs. real incident)
- Whether the client's security team / SOC is aware of the engagement

## 5. Emergency Procedures (5 min)

- Review emergency stop contacts from the ROE
- Confirm both contacts are reachable during testing hours
- Confirm what triggers an emergency stop
- Confirm what happens if rmsecurity finds evidence of an active third-party breach

## 6. Deliverables and Timeline (10 min)

- Confirm report delivery date
- Confirm report format (PDF, DOCX, both)
- Confirm report recipients (names, roles, email addresses)
- Confirm debrief call — schedule it now
- Discuss retest window and process

## 7. Questions (10 min)

- Open floor for client questions
- Clarify any remaining scope or technical questions

## 8. Wrap-Up

- Confirm testing start time (exact)
- Confirm testing start notification will be sent
- Confirm emergency contacts are valid
- Thank client and close
