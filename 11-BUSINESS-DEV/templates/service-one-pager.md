## Our Approach

**Manual testing, not scanner output.**
Automated tools find known CVEs. We find the logic flaws, chained vulnerabilities, and business risks that scanners miss.

**OWASP · PTES · MITRE ATT&CK · NIST SP 800-61**
Every engagement follows established methodology. Every finding is confirmed before it appears in your report.

**Four quality gates per engagement.**
Scope approval → Testing complete → Report reviewed → Delivery confirmed.
Nothing moves forward without explicit sign-off at each stage.
