## Scope Adjustment Factors

| Factor | Adjustment |
|--------|-----------|
| Scope larger than standard | +20–40% |
| Accelerated timeline (< 2 weeks lead time) | +25% |
| Weekend or after-hours testing required | +30% |
| Travel required (on-site) | Cost + travel expenses |
| Retest (outside included window) | 30% of original engagement price |
| Report in Spanish + English | +10% |

| Situation | Discount |
|---------|---------|
| Multi-engagement contract (3+) | 10% |

Discounts are not cumulative without explicit approval.

---

## Payment Terms

- 50% upfront upon contract signature
- 50% upon report delivery
- Net 15 days from invoice date
- Late payment: 1.5% per month after 30 days
- Retainers: billed monthly in advance

---

## Expenses

Travel, accommodation, and tools/licenses required specifically for
the engagement are billed at cost with receipts.
Standard tooling (Burp Suite, Nessus, BloodHound) is included in day rate.
