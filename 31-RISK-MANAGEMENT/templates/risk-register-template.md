## Scoring Guide

**Likelihood:**
| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 1 | Rare — unlikely given current controls |
| 2 | Unlikely — possible but not expected |
| 3 | Possible — could occur under certain conditions |
| 4 | Likely — expected to occur if not addressed |
| 5 | Almost certain — will occur without immediate action |

**Impact:**
| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 1 | Negligible — minimal operational disruption |
| 2 | Minor — limited impact, recoverable quickly |
| 3 | Moderate — significant disruption, days to recover |
| 4 | Major — severe disruption, weeks to recover, possible regulatory action |
| 5 | Catastrophic — existential threat, major data breach, business continuity failure |

**Risk Score = Likelihood × Impact (1–25)**
- 20–25: Critical
- 12–19: High
- 6–11: Medium
- 1–5: Low
