# rmsecurity — Color Palette

Extracted from the primary logo. These values are authoritative for all
reports, templates, presentations, and future digital products.

---

## Primary Colors

| Name | HEX | RGB | Use |
|------|-----|-----|-----|
| Navy Black | `#0A0F1E` | 10, 15, 30 | Page backgrounds, dark sections |
| Deep Navy | `#0D1B2A` | 13, 27, 42 | Report headers, primary dark surfaces |
| Brand Blue | `#1E6FD9` | 30, 111, 217 | Primary accent — headings, links, highlights |
| Electric Blue | `#4DA3FF` | 77, 163, 255 | Secondary accent — callouts, badges |
| Silver | `#C0C8D4` | 192, 200, 212 | Secondary text, dividers, icon fills |
| White | `#F4F6F9` | 244, 246, 249 | Body text on dark, light backgrounds |

## Semantic Colors (for reports and findings)

| Low | `#2E7D32` | Low severity findings |

## Gradients (for presentations only, not reports)

| Name | Direction | From | To |
|------|-----------|------|----|
| Shield gradient | Top-left → Bottom-right | `#1E6FD9` | `#0D1B2A` |
| Silver gradient | Top → Bottom | `#E8EDF2` | `#9BAAB8` |

---

## Usage Rules

- Never use pure black (`#000000`) — use `#0A0F1E` instead
- Never use pure white (`#FFFFFF`) on reports — use `#F4F6F9`
- Brand Blue (`#1E6FD9`) is the only accent color on report covers
- Severity colors are reserved exclusively for finding classification
- Gradients are for PowerPoint decks only — PDFs and DOCX use flat fills
