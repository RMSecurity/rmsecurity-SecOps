# rmsecurity — Brand Guidelines

## Identity Statement

**rmsecurity** is a professional cybersecurity consulting firm.
The brand communicates precision, trust, and technical authority.
Every client-facing document must reinforce these values.

**Tagline:** Assess · Protect · Respond · Improve

The tagline maps directly to the four operational phases of every engagement:
- **Assess** — understand the current security posture
- **Protect** — implement controls and hardening
- **Respond** — contain and eradicate threats
- **Improve** — close the loop with metrics and continuous improvement

---

## Logo Rules

### Do
- Use the primary logo (dark background version) on report covers
- Use the light background version on white report pages
- Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the "R" letterform on all sides
- Scale proportionally — never stretch or distort

### Do not
- Change the colors of the logo
- Add drop shadows or glow effects
- Place the logo on a busy background without a containing shape
- Use the logo at sizes smaller than 80px wide (digital) or 2cm wide (print)
- Rotate or flip the logo

---

## Typography

| Use | Typeface | Weight | Size |
|-----|---------|--------|------|
| Report title | Montserrat or equivalent | 700 Bold | 28–36pt |
| Section headings | Montserrat | 600 SemiBold | 16–20pt |
| Body text | Calibri or equivalent | 400 Regular | 11–12pt |
| Code / technical content | Consolas or Courier New | 400 Regular | 10pt |
| Captions | Calibri | 400 Italic | 9pt |

If Montserrat is unavailable, substitute with: **Arial**, **Segoe UI**, or **Open Sans**.

---

## Report Cover Standard

Every report cover must contain:
1. rmsecurity logo (top-left or centered)
2. Report type (e.g., "Penetration Test Report")
3. Client name or engagement code
4. Date of report
5. Confidentiality classification
6. Tagline (optional, bottom of cover)

Background: Deep Navy (`#0D1B2A`) or white — never gray.

---

## Confidentiality Classifications

Every document must be classified. Use one of:

| Label | Meaning | Color |
|-------|---------|-------|
| `CONFIDENTIAL` | Client-specific — do not share outside engagement | Red banner |
| `RESTRICTED` | Internal rmsecurity use only | Orange banner |
| `INTERNAL` | rmsecurity team, no external distribution | Yellow banner |
| `PUBLIC` | May be shared publicly (e.g., blog posts) | No banner |

The classification appears in the footer of every page and on the cover.

---

## Watermarking

Draft reports must be watermarked with `DRAFT — NOT FOR DISTRIBUTION`
diagonally across every page.

Final delivered reports must be watermarked with the client name or engagement ID
at 10% opacity across every page.
