## Key Metrics

### Business Health
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)** — retainer contracts
- **Pipeline Value** — weighted by stage probability
- **Win Rate** — proposals won / proposals sent
- **Average Engagement Value** — total revenue / engagement count
- **Utilization Rate** — billable hours / available hours

### Delivery Quality
- **Report On-Time Rate** — reports delivered by committed date
- **Gate Failure Rate** — engagements that failed a quality gate
- **Findings per Engagement** — average by service type
- **Retest Pass Rate** — findings resolved on first retest
- **Client NPS** — net promoter score from post-engagement survey

### Security Intelligence
- **CVEs Triaged per Month** — from monitoring procedure
- **KB Findings Added per Quarter** — knowledge base growth
- **Playbook Updates per Quarter** — methodology currency
